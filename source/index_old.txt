  <div class="large-8 columns push-4">
    <p>
      In my current research I explore the notion of the cyborg plant. I am interested to see what happens when the vegetable is augmented with technology.
    </p>
    <p>
      Some questions I have are:
    </p>
    <ul class="resume-bullets">
      <li>Can plants make decisions?
      </li>
      <li>Can they show preference?
      </li>
      <li>Could the plant perspective be translated to human?
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      I approach this process by using the tools of science and engineering but most importantly by looking through the lens of art.
                </p>
